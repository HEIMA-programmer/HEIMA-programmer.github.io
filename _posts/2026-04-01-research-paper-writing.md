### Abstract Writing 
The abstract can be divided into four parts: 
- Two sentences describing the biological background, for example: RNA plays an important role in biological regulation, so the positioning of RNA-targeted drugs has become an important research topic. 
- A one-sentence summary of existing methodological limitations, such as the challenges of fusing multi-level structured data 
- Describe the model and methods of this paper 
- Finally, sufficient experiments prove that this method outperforms other existing methods, especially under what circumstances

### Introduction Writing
The Introduction part can be divided into four paragraphs:
- The first paragraph specifically explains the biological background and demonstrates the importance of this topic through specific cases. 
- The second paragraph explains the limitations of wet experiments, and then introduces existing methods, starting from traditional methods to existing deep learning methods, methods migrated from other fields, etc. 
- The third paragraph explains that although existing methods have achieved many results, there are still many challenges. List them one by one and give examples of the methods. 
- The fourth paragraph explains what framework and modules this method proposes to address these challenges. The final experiment proves that it is the best and performs particularly well in certain experimental settings that are consistent with real-life scenarios.
