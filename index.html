About 关于我

I am a junior undergraduate student at the Dundee International Institute, Central South University (CSU), majoring in Computer Science & Technology. I have been selected for the CSU Undergraduate-to-PhD Elite Program, advised by Prof. Lei Deng from the School of Computer Science & Engineering.

My research interest lies in bioinformatics, with a current focus on non-coding RNA, drug resistance prediction, and the application of deep learning methods in life sciences. I am always open to discussions and collaborations — feel free to reach out!

我是中南大学邓迪国际学院计算机科学与技术专业的大三本科生，入选了中南大学本博拔尖培养计划，师从计算机学院邓磊教授。

我的研究方向是生物信息学，目前专注于非编码RNA与药物耐药性预测等课题，探索深度学习方法在生命科学领域的应用。欢迎有兴趣交流或合作的同学联系我！
